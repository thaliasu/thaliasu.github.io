IMITATE Graphic Design

Background

Users of IMITATE are able to view lesson content and download attached media, such as audio, documents, or presentations. We needed to a way to utilize empty space and convey the type of media that could be downloaded.

Early Designs

My early sketches tended to be a bit too complex. When I put the graphics together in Illustrator, I simplified features and made several versions.

Final Designs

To maintain consistency, I used the same circle background and rounded corners. The graphic filled up blank space and drew eyes to the call-to-action.
